About

What is the heroes project?

The heroes project is a website carried out by students of the master course in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge at the university of Bologna. It is meant to be a trans-historical, typographical travel in time. We chose some historic heroes to guide us through a path from the VIII Century AD to the furthest idea of future we had in mind!
