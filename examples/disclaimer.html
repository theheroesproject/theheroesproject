Purpose

This project means to explore various types of typographic styles for text documents, as an exam for the "Information Modeling and Web technologies" course of the Master Degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge of the University of Bologna, under prof. Fabio Vitali.

The documents contained in this web site have been selected for their length and complexity among the best picks of 2018 of www.longreads.com. Their publication here is not intended to be an alternative or replace their original locations:

  1. Bloomberg: Japan's Prisons Are a Haven for Elderly Women
  2. The Cut: Maybe She Had So Much Money She Just Lost Track of It
  3. Harpers: As Goes the South, so Goes the Nation
  4. Times Literary Supplement: God is in the machine
  5. Huffington Post: Jerry and Marge Go Large
  6. EUDirective: Directive 2009/41/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council
